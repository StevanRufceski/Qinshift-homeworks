WHO WE ARE

VALUES that we create

  • PRO - consultancy services from experts for managing bids for tenders, and managing procurement for projects.
  • TEND - database of public calls for procurement announced by state authorities (SA), private companies (PC) and international financial agencies (IFA).
  • SUB - database of active participants in the tenders, from each industry, willing to join ventures.

BENEFITS that we provide

  • For BASIC PARTICIPANTS ON TENDERS, cheap and efficient participation on tenders.
  • For PROACTIVE PARTICIPANTS ON TENDERS, reduced liability and costs for tendering.
  • For PASSIVE PARTICIPANTS ON TENDERS, increased effectiveness on projects.
  • For STATE AUTHORITIES, legal and successful complex procurements.
